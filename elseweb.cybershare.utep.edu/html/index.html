Welcome to ELSEWeb

The Earth, Life and Semantic Web (ELSeWeb) project integrates the NASA-funded Earth Data Analysis Center (EDAC, http://edac.unm.edu/) with an analytical Web Service platform, Lifemapper, (Lifemapper.org) which models potential future species distributions under scenarios of climate change.
